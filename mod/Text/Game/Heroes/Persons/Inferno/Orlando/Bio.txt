Ever charismatic, cunning, and ruthless, Orlando quickly grew in power, wealth, and status under the new regime imposed by Queen Isabel after her return from Sheogh. Rumors say that his close allies benefit from strange enchantments, the receptions he hosts are places of unseen debauchery, and his political enemies tend to suffer painful deaths. Rumors also say that one should not repeat them aloud in a company one doesn't entirely trust, lest one disappears in dubious circumstances.
