Ever since his youth, Xerxon has been a sadistic bully. He takes great pleasure in tormenting and hurting those around him, and he is never so happy as he is in the middle of battle, which led him to being a mercenary for a long time, even acquiring some renown as one. Sensing his body begin to falter, he offered himself to become a Death Knight of Heresh as a means to continue his tumultous life.
