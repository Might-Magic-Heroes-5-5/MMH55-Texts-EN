The wife of an up-and-coming Elven noble, silver-haired Ashbeth never found her place at King Arniel's court. Her favourite topic of gossip was Tuidhana, the rebellious Queen of Tarlad, then a scandalous figure in Elven society. This fascination slowly grew into an obsession. Already drifting away from her family, Ashbeth decided to betray her own people and stood with the Dark Elves after the Day of the Tears of Fire, eventually joining them in their subterranean exile.
