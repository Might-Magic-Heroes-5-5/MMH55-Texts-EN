Activates special adventure abilities if enabled by the user and provides additional info about the hero.<br><br><color=orange>Town Gate:<color=white> Can be activated anywhere on the adventure map. Teleports the caster and his army to the garrison of any owned town of choice.<br><br><color=orange>Town Conversion:<color=white> Can be activated if hero is standing in the gate (not in the garrison) of a town of a different faction. Costs will depend on Town level.<br><br><color=orange>Town Governor:<color=white> The hero can be inaugurated as governor when standing in the gate (not in the garrison) of a town of his own faction.
