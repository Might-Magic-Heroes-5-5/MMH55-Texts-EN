The hero constructs a Wand artifact for personal use and adds it to his inventory. The hero is also granted the ability to dissolve artifacts equipped on friendly creatures to use the magical energy they contain to heal and resurrect them in combat.<br><color_default>The number of Hit Points healed is equal to 20 * (Hero Level) * (1 + X), where X is the number of effects crafted in the dissolved artifact. The hero spends 33% of his turn to dissolve the artifact. Only a single wand can be made per hero.
