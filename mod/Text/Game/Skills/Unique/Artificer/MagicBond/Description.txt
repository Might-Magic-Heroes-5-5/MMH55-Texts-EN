The hero gains the ability to place their mark on any friendly or enemy stack. Each subsequent targeted spell striking that target has its effect doubled and costs twice as much mana. Moreover, every spell targeting another creature will affect this target as well.<br><color_default>The caster can be bound to only one target at a time. If there is not enough mana, the effect is not doubled. The hero's ATB value is reset to 0.24 after choosing the target.
