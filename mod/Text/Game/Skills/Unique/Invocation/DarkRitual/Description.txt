Adventure Skill: The hero can perform a ritual to regain full mana at the cost of movement points. Depending on the mastery of Sorcery, the hero will lose 14, 12 or 10 movement points for every point of mana recovered.<color_default><br>For example, at expert sorcery the hero will lose the equivalent of 1 tile worth of movement for every 10 Mana recovered. This ability can only be used when the hero hasn't lost any movement points.
