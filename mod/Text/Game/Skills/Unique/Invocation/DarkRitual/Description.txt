Adventure Skill: The hero can perform a ritual to regain full mana at the cost of movement points. Depending on the mastery of Sorcery, the hero will lose 12, 10 or 8 movement points for every point of mana recovered.<color_default><br>For example, at expert sorcery the hero will lose the equivalent of 1 tile worth of movement for every 10 Mana recovered. This ability can only be used when the hero hasn't lost any movement points.
