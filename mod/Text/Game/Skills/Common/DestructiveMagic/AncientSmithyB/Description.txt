The hero gains the Fireball spell, +3 Spellpower and all creatures led by the hero will inflict additional Fire damage equal to 10% of the damage inflicted by their attacks. Enemies who are Immune to Fire suffer no additional damage. The hero becomes a pariah among his own kind resulting in a -1 Morale penalty.<color_default><br>The morale penalty is permanent, even a memory mentor will not be able to remove it.
