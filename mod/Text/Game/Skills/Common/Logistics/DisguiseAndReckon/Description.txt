The hero controls the flow of information in conquered territory and hides information from their enemies. Whenever a friendly hero anywhere on the map learns a new spell, rune or warcry the hero will also learn it the next day if they have the required magic skills. Enemies will see only the strongest creature in the hero's army with no number at all unless they have the scouting ability. The hero can also see the courage of neutrals.<br><color_default>Spells are not gained from friendly heroes who have a tome equipped containing all spells of a school.
