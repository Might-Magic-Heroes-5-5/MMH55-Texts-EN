Hero's Attack attribute increases the damage inflicted by might attacks of the hero army by effectively increasing their Attack attribute in combat. It also improves the effectiveness of the Ballista.<br><br><color_default>Damage Calculation: If A is larger than D, inflicted Damage = AssaultingStack_Size * Random(AssaultingStack_MinDamage, AssaultingStack_MaxDamage) * [1 + 0.025*(A-D)]; if D is larger than A, inflicted Damage = AssaultingStack_Size * Random(AssaultingStack_MinDamage, AssaultingStack_MaxDamage) / [1 + 0.025*(D-A)].
