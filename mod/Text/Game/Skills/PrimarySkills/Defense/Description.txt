Hero's Defense attribute decreases the damage suffered by the hero army from enemy creatures' might attacks by effectively increasing their Defense attribute in combat.<br><br><color_default>Damage Calculation: If A is larger than D, inflicted Damage = AssaultingStack_Size * Random(AssaultingStack_MinDamage, AssaultingStack_MaxDamage) * [1 + 0.025*(A-D)]; if D is larger than A, inflicted Damage = AssaultingStack_Size * Random(AssaultingStack_MinDamage, AssaultingStack_MaxDamage) / [1 + 0.025*(D-A)].
