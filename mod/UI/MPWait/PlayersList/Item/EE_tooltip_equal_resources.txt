<color=green>Casual Mode<br><color_default>
* The AI does not receive any extra creatures or gold to reduce losses in 'Quick Combat'.
* AI Starting resources are reduced by 10000/5/10 on all difficulty levels.
* AI Players are not allies but their mutual aggression drops on higher difficulties.
* AI heroes are allowed to flee.
* Battles sites have -10% growth.
<br><color=white>Warning!<br><color_default>
* Do not use 'restart mission' after the map is loaded or the AI will revert to NOT casual, sometimes even one of the very hard modes!
