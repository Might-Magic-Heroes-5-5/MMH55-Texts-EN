There can be one Governor per town only and must be from the native faction hero list. Governors must remain within a 50 tile radius around this Town otherwise he will lose more and more movement points the further he goes, also any bonuses granted by the town will be removed. Governors lower than level 5 also lose some movement points when close to the town. The hero is obligated to govern this town for at least one month, if the hero decides to resign earlier he must pay a fine to the towns inhabitants equal to the amount of days not in office*his level*100. The hero is not alowed to use "Town Gate" ability to teleport to other towns.
